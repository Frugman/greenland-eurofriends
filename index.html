Greenland Penpals

We are collecting postcards with messages of support for the people of Greenland to send in packages to nursing homes, cultural centers, schools, and city halls there, so they can be distributed to the local residents.

Buy or create a postcard from where you live, write a simple message, or copy one of the templates in the presentation above. Do it with your children, your friends, and your loved ones, then place the cards in an envelope to be sent to the address shown here.

Please hurry, as we would like to ship them out in time for February 14th (Friends' Day).
